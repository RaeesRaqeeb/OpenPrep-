How to contribute
No experience needed. Pick whichever fits you — even one question helps the next student who needs it.
01
Add MCQs
Submit questions with explanations for any exam. Follow the format or share your ideas.
02
Fix errors
Spot a wrong answer or typo? Open a GitHub issue or submit a fix directly to our spot error page .
03
Write code
Frontend, backend, or UI improvements. Check open issues tagged "good first issue".
04
Spread the word
Share OpenPrep in your university WhatsApp groups. More students = more contributors.
