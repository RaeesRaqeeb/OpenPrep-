About OpenPrep

Prep that's open
to everyone

OpenPrep exists for one reason — no Pakistani student should miss out on their dream university because they couldn't afford the prep.

Our mission

Premium prep.
Zero cost.

"Every student — regardless of where they're from or what they can afford — deserves a real shot at IBA, LUMS, FAST, NUST, NED and beyond."

OpenPrep is a free platform where students preparing for university admission tests find the best and most proven preparation material — carefully built and verified by students who already got through those same tests.

Prep academies and coaching centers charge tens of thousands of rupees for the same material. We think that's wrong. So we built OpenPrep and made it free forever.

Open source

Built by the community,
for the community

OpenPrep is open source and openly invites every Pakistani university student to be part of it. You don't need to be a developer or an expert — just the willingness to help the next batch of students the way someone helped you.

Write code
Help build features, fix bugs, or improve the platform on GitHub.
Add questions
Contribute MCQs with explanations to any exam's question bank.
Give feedback
Spot an error or confusing explanation? Open an issue and tell us.
Mentor students
If you scored well, help us identify and personally guide students who are struggling.
Personalized study

We track your progress
so you don't have to guess

OpenPrep doesn't just hand you questions and leave you wondering how you're doing. It keeps a record of every quiz you attempt — how many you got right, how many you got wrong, and which papers you've already covered.

Your personal dashboard shows exactly where you stand so you can spend your time fixing weaknesses — not just repeating what you already know.

OpenPrep will always be free for students. But running it has real costs — a domain name, hosting, and storage all add up. Right now that comes entirely out of pocket.

If OpenPrep helped you — or you believe in what it stands for — we'd genuinely appreciate any contribution towards keeping it online. Even a small amount makes a real difference.
